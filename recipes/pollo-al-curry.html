Pollo al Curry

Description

Chicken breasts marinated with curry powder and cooked inside double-cream sauce. Usually accompanied by a serving of rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken breasts
  • 1 Cup of double cream
  • 1/2 Cup of Tomato paste
  • 1 Cup of Rice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Lemon juice
  1. Rinse the onions,garlic and chicken breasts.
  2. Cut the chicken breasts into cubes.
  3. Put the rice in a salted pot for 15-20 minutes until soft.
  4. Season the cubes with lemon juice, salt, pepper and a minced garlic clove, leave it marinating in the fridge for a few minutes.
  5. Toss the onions onto an oiled up pan on low heat until they look translucent.
  6. Add a teaspoon of curry powder and 1/2 cup of tomato paste, leave 5 minutes on low heat.
  7. Add the chicken breasts cubes and cook for 15 minutes, stir with a wooden spoon every few minutes.
  8. Take the rice out and strain if there's still water on the pot.
  9. When the chicken is done, add the double cream and season to your liking, let it cook for another 10-15 minutes.

Done!
